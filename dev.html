About us

Dax Animations Inc. is a well-known computer graphics animation developing company with its headquarters in California, USA. The core business of the company is to create animation and high-end professional graphics in many Hollywood movies. An animation studio is a company producing animated media. The broadest such companies conceive of products to produce, own the physical equipment for production, employ operators for that equipment, and hold a major stake in the sales or rentals of the media produced.
